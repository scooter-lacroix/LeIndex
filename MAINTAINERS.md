## Becoming a Maintainer

Active contributors who demonstrate consistent quality contributions and engagement with the project may be invited to become maintainers.

### Criteria for Maintainership

1. **Consistent Contributions**: At least 5 high-quality pull requests merged
2. **Code Review**: Active participation in reviewing pull requests
3. **Issue Triage**: Help manage and respond to issues
4. **Documentation**: Contributions to documentation and examples
5. **Community Engagement**: Helpful and constructive interactions

### Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage and respond to issues
- Maintain code quality and test coverage
- Update documentation as needed
- Follow the project's coding conventions
- Participate in architectural decisions
